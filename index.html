Strategic Digital Defense, LLC Leadership

Strategic Digital Defense is led by David T. Klein, Chief Architect and General Manager.

David is a cybersecurity architect and counter-espionage consultant. He specializes in how cryptography and cryptographic assets and technologies fit into the core business of the client organization, and the lifecycle surrounding these resources.

David brings five years of experience in designing, deploying and managing cryptographic assets and technologies. These assets include cryptographic keys and certificates. The technologies include hardware bulk encryptors (hardware security modules and encryption servers), Enterprise Key and Certificate Management Systems and PKI.

With ten years of experience at USAA, the world-class financial services firm, and consulting engagements including a Fortune 100 international airline, David brings best-in-class tools, techniques and leadership to help you defend what matters most.
